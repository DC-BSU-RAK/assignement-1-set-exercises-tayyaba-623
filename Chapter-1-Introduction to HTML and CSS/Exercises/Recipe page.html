Chicken Spaghetti

Description Ingredients Methods

Description

Chicken spaghetti is a mouthwatering dish that combines juicy, tender chicken, flavorful sauce, and a soft omelet. The creamy sauce creates a satisfying
experience. It's perfect for any event, whether you cook it for family or serve it at a gathering.


Ingredients

  • One pack of spaghetti
  • Cooking oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 kg chicken
  • 1 medium-sized carrot
  • 1 medium-sized capsicum
  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon chilli sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Ketchup
  • 3 Eggs

Method

  1. Fill a pot with water, add one teaspoon of salt and one tablespoon of oil, and place the pot on a high flame. When the water starts boiling, add the spaghetti and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  2. While the spaghetti is boiling, fill another pot with water, add the chicken, and cook until it is fully cooked. After boiling, chop the chicken into small pieces.
  3. Next, chop the garlic and slice the onion, carrot, capsicum, and cauliflower.
  4. Now, prepare the omelet. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add salt and black pepper, then stir the eggs with a whisk or a fork. Pour the eggs into a pan and cook them. Once cooked, cut the omelet into small pieces.
  5. Add two tablespoons of oil to a pot and heat it. Fry the chopped garlic, then add the onion and fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the chicken and cook until it turns golden brown. After stirring the chicken, add the cauliflower, capsicum, and carrot. Cook until the chicken and all the vegetables are cooked well.
  7. Add all the spices (salt, black pepper, chilli sauce, soy sauce, and vinegar).
  8. Once the spaghetti is boiled properly. Drain it, pour it into the mixture of chicken and vegetables, and stir.
  9. Finally, add ketchup and the omelet pieces to the spaghetti.
  10. Now the spaghetti is ready to enjoy with a cold drink.
